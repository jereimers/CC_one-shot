Encounter: Casino Royale Car — High-Stakes Poker

Type: Social/Skill Challenge
Themes: Deception, bluffing, social manipulation, cheating

Setup:
- Lavish casino car with NPC high-rollers.
- Players get 3 “Chips of Fate”; they may win by bluff, skill, or subterfuge.

Mechanics:
- 3-Round Skill Challenge with escalating DCs (15 → 17 → 18).
- Primary Skills: Deception, Insight, Sleight of Hand, Intimidation, Performance, Persuasion.
- Failure can summon animated dealers ("Living Deck" stat block).
- NPCs have quirks, tells, and flaws (see cue card handout).

Reward: Secret info, golden train token, leverage.
