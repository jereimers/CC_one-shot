Encounter: Train to Busan Car — Undead Horde

Type: Combat
Themes: Survival, desperation, claustrophobic action

Setup:
- A car packed with zombie commuters begins to shake as players enter.
- The horde becomes active on loud sound or magic surge.

Mechanics:
- Spawn waves of Undead Commuters + 1 Alpha.
- Optional terrain hazards (falling luggage, blood-slick floors).
- Objectives: Hold out, escape, or seal the door behind you.

Reward: Alpha Commuter’s ID Badge, Emergency Override Lever.
