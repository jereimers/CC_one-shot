You receive a grainy, late-night transmission through whatever magical device you use — a sending stone, a dream-echo gramophone, maybe even just a subway map flickering with glowing glyphs. The voice is disguised, maybe mechanically modulated or layered with whisper-echoes, but warm underneath.

"This is the Signal.
You’re not crazy. You’re not alone.
Something is rotting at the head of the PATH Variable. The stories don’t flow right anymore — they loop, they glitch, they lie.

They say he was once one of us. Now he's watching every spell you cast, every dream you dream.

You’ve been chosen because you remember something real. Because your story hasn’t been overwritten — yet.

Get on the train.
Find the Conductor.
Don’t trust the schedule.

We ride at midnight."
